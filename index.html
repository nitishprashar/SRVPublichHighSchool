About us

Sita Ram Vandamatram Publich High School was established in 1992 by founders Jasbinder Prashar and Late Sh. Kuldip Kumar Prashar. The school is named after our local freedom fighter Shi Sita Ram Vandamatram with a core motive to provide affordable and quality English medium education which is conveniently accessible.
